# Design Context

## Users
- General public in Thailand checking heatwave conditions
- People planning travel or outdoor activities
- Context: Morning/evening checks, mobile-first usage

## Brand Personality
- Warm but professional
- 3 words: **Reassuring, Precise, Essential**

## Aesthetic Direction
- Light theme - bright, clean, trustworthy
- AI dashboard aesthetic - minimal, data-forward, calm
- Like weather.com or Apple's weather app but with AI feel
- White/light gray background with warm orange/red accents for heat indicators

## Design Principles
1. **Clarity first** - Temperature data is the hero
2. **Calm palette** - Light backgrounds, subtle shadows, no harsh colors
3. **Progressive disclosure** - Show key info immediately, details on hover
4. **Regional focus** - Thailand provinces as primary organization
5. **Visual hierarchy** - Temperature numbers prominent, context secondary
